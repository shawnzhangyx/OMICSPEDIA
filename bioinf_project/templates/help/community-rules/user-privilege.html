User privileges

Vote

Users are strongly encouraged to vote on the post or software that they liked. Vote can be cast on any post(question, discussion, blog and replies), comment and software. Votes carry a lot of weight in helping others to identify good materials, though requires minimal efforts (only a double click!).

Bookmark

Wiki pages, tags, software, and posts are bookmarkable. Users can view their bookmarks in their profiles. If a tag(software) is bookmarked, user will receive updates from that tag (such as new posts, new wiki pages with that tag); if a wiki page is bookmarked, users will be notified by new revisions; if a post is bookmarked, users will have access to new answers or comments to that post. The notifications are located on the top bar. Users can also get e-mail updates in addition to the notifications. This can be changed in the Userprofile>Settings.

User privilege levels

  1. Anonymous users
    • can view all contents
  2. Registered users:
    • own a user profile
    • bookmark wiki, tags, posts
    • vote
  3. E-mail verified users:
    • create wiki, tags, software, posts, comments
    • edit wiki, tags, software, posts
    • edit your own comments
  4. Moderators:
    • Moderate
  5. Blocked users:
    • Not able to log in.
    • If you're blocked and want to restore your account, please contact us.

Daily action limitations

To encourage high-quality posts, comments and votes, we set limitations on the actions a user could perform every day. subject to change

  • No more than 10 questions within 24 hours
  • No more than 10 answers within 24 hours
  • No more than 10 replies (in discussion) within an hour
  • No more than 20 comments within 24 hours
  • No more than 20 votes within 24 hours
